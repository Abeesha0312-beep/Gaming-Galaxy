Evolution of Gaming

Explore how gaming evolved through decades

1970s

The birth of gaming with arcade classics like Pong and Space Invaders.

1980s

The golden age with Pac-Man, Mario Bros, and rise of consoles like Atari.

1990s

Introduction of 3D gaming with PlayStation and titles like Doom, Quake, and Zelda.

2000s

Online multiplayer rises with Halo, Counter-Strike, and MMORPGs.

2010s

Explosion of mobile and indie games. eSports and streaming boom.

2020s

Cloud gaming, virtual reality and the metaverse begin shaping the future.
